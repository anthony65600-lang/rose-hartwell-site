Rose Hartwell

Practical log books & trackers

Rose Hartwell writes practical, easy-to-use log books and trackers designed to make everyday health and home record-keeping simple. Every book is built around large print, clear tables, and real information people actually need - whether that's tracking blood pressure at home, keeping a car's full service history, or organizing a family's medical visits. Rose believes good tools should get out of your way: no clutter, no complicated systems, just a clear place to write things down.
